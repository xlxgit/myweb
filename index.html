About me

I received the B.Sc. degree in Chemistry from Shandong Universityi (SDU) in 2011 and PhD degree from the University of Hong Kong (HKU) in 2016. Then I worked as Research Assistant at the University of Hong Kong (HKU) under the supervision of Prof. Kwong-Yu Chan in 2017. During 2017-2018, I worked in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) under the supervision of Prof. Xuhui Huang.

Research

My research interests include

  • Multi-scale molecular modeling

  • Machine Learning

  • Computer aided drug discovery

  • Bioinformatics
